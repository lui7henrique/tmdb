**Plotwist** is a complete platform for managing and reviewing movies, series, anime, dramas, and more, all in one place, unlike alternatives like Letterboxd and MyAnimeList.

#### **Free Plan**
- Unlimited reviews and lists.  
- Tools to manage watched, ongoing, and planned content.  
- Collaborative lists and recommendations for friends.  

#### **Pro Plan**
- Detailed statistics (hours watched, genres, actors, etc.).  
- Personalized recommendations.  
- Badges and achievements.  
- Early access to new features.  

#### **Objective**
Build a vibrant community and become the ultimate destination for audiovisual entertainment enthusiasts.
